About Greenfield University

Founded in 1965, Greenfield University has grown into one of the most respected institutions in the region. With over 20,000 students from 80 countries, we offer a diverse and inclusive learning environment. Our mission is to advance knowledge, foster creativity, and prepare graduates who make a positive impact on the world.
